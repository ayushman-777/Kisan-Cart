Our Idea(KisanCart) propose to create a system where farmers and traders can sell and purchase products outside ‘mandis’. Our idea also encourage intra-state trade and propose to reduce transportation cost. The idea formulate a framework on agreements that enables farmers to engage with agri-business companies, exporters and retailers for services and sale of produce while giving the farmer access to modern technology.​.

As farmers start to trade with KisanCart eventually they will develop trust that they will get buyers for any produce rather than traditional crops.​

Our idea also include to educate and make farmers aware by telling them about different policies of government through regular notification by SMS and calling them.​ This is especially fruitful for small and marginal Indian farmers who often lack necessary information regarding government scheme that are beneficial for them to get compensation for their losses due to bad weather.​
